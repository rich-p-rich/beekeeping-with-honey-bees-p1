There are over 20,000 known species of bee in the world, from bumblebees to leafcutter bees to mason bees. But beekeeping involves keeping hives full of the Honey Bee, or Apis Mellifera to give it its Latin name.


Honey Bees live in colonies of 20,000 to 80,000 depending on the time of the year and strength of the colony. There are three types of Honey Bees: the queen, the worker bees and the drones.
